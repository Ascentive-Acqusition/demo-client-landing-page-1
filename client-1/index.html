From McKinsey
to meaning.

I grew up in Chiang Mai, Thailand. Ivy League. Google. McKinsey. C-suite with an 8-figure P&L. Every box, ticked. On the outside.

Inside, I was collapsing. The always-on pressure forced me into total burnout and a 6-month medical leave. That crisis became my catalyst.

I stopped following the Default Script. I returned to McKinsey, got promoted to Associate Partner, then landed my next C-suite role — all on my own terms.
